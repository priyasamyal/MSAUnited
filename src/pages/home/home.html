ABOUT HUB

There are several progressive brain diseases that are also characterized as sporadic such as Multiple System Atrophy, Cortical Basal Degeneration, Progressive Supranuclear Palsy, among others. Sporadic diseases are not confined to certain geographies, populations or demographics. As progressive disabling conditions, people suffering from these kinds of diseases face insurmountable challenges to attend regular onsite support meetings. The Global Online Support Hub (GOSH) was designed as an alternative community resource to connect patients with progressive brain diseases and their caregivers with others who find themselves in a similar situation.
